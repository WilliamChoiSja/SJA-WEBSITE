My Work Experience

From 2015 to 2017 I worked in the American International school of Chunai as a teaching assistant in the library. I helped elementary and middle school kids find resources for their units of Inquiry. It was during this time period where I became curious about makerspaces.


From 2017 to 2019 I worked as a Assistant Librarian and as a media specialist Librarian in Saudi Arabia. I helped teachers intergrate technology into their lessons during as a media specialist Libarian. Furthermore, it was during this time period where I started to begin hosting makerspaces during lunchtime and afterschool In order to support student interests and passions


From 2019 to 2022, I worked in Vietnam as a ICT Teacher and as a Media Specalist Librarian. I taught research skills and hosted digital citizenship lessons for students ranging from Elementary school to High school. I also worked as a debate coach for 9th/10th graders during my spare time. Finally, it was during this time where I was Recognized for my work in Vietnam by the German Agency of International Issues and it was published in their 2021 Feburary Magezine.


From 2022 to 2025, I worked in South Korea, Jeju. I worked as a Steam, Robotics, Computer Science, and Engineering Teacher. I began 8 elective courses for students, developing various Steam and Robotics programs for them. Furthermore, I was a assistant coach for Middle and High School Robotics, and I was the head coach for the table tennis teams. Finally, I was a core advisor for NHS society for 2 years.
